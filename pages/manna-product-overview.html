What's Inside

Six features.
One purpose.

Every feature in Manna is built around building healthy community, all features are built around dignity, privacy and love for one another.

Community Wall

A curated content feed where leaders can post spiritual resources, guides, programme materials, videos, articles, and announcements for their community.

Supports video, audio, documents, external links, and Spotify embeds. Content is filtered by role so each user sees what is relevant to them. Org Admins and Partners can post. Guests and alumni can view and engage.

Daily Bread

A daily devotional served to the entire community. Each entry includes a scripture reading, reflection, and a closing prayer written by the zanmanna editorial team.

365 devotionals curated and managed globally by zanmanna. Automatically served by date. Includes a "Read Full Chapter" shortcut linked to the built-in Bible. If Org Admins prefer their own 365 devotional content this can be arranged on request.

Private Journal

A completely private journaling space. Voice-to-text enabled. Each entry belongs only to the person who wrote it. No admin can access any user's journal, ever.

Privacy enforced at the data layer using Row Owners. Zero admin visibility under any circumstance, including Global Admin. Each journal entry supports title, mood, written or voice-transcribed text, and an optional image attachment. Great for personal devotion and reflection.

Prayer Wall

A shared prayer board where community members lift each other up. Anonymous by default. Hearts indicate solidarity. Answered prayers are celebrated and marked.

Users can post, edit, mark as answered, and delete their own requests. Admins can leave pastoral comments visible only to the poster. Full anonymity control is in the hands of the user, not the admin. Prayer categories help filter by type.

Full Bible

The complete Word of God. All 66 books, 1,189 chapters, and 31,103 verses in the World English Bible translation. Always available, fully offline-capable.

Navigable by Testament, book, and chapter. Full text search. Chapter-level deep linking from devotionals. No internet connection required to read. Scripture is never gated, never shortened, never monetised.

Emergency Help

One tap connects a community member to immediate pastoral support. A crisis request is logged and an email is sent instantly to the designated admin for their organisation.

Users reach a "You are not alone" reassurance screen with colour-coded emergency call buttons. Numbers are configured per organisation and pulled dynamically, covering crisis helplines, backup lines, and local emergency services. The admin receives a reply-to email linked directly to the person in need.

How It Works

One app. Every organisation. Fully isolated.

Manna runs on a multi-tenant architecture. Every organisation gets the same powerful platform, but all data is strictly isolated. Your community only ever sees their own content.

zanmanna manages the platform globally. You manage your community locally. Both streams appear to users as one seamless, unified experience.

Global Content (zanmanna)

Daily devotionals and curated resources managed by the zanmanna editorial team. Updated once, instantly available to all organisations.

Org Content (your team)

Your announcements, resources, and pastoral comments. Visible only to your organisation's members. Fully controlled by your Org Admin.

Join Code Onboarding

New members join with a 6-character code you share privately. No public registration. No open access. You control who enters your community.

Platform

iOS and Android. Built on Glide. No app store installation required. Accessible via a shared link, just like a website.

User Roles

The right access.
For every person.

Every person in your community has a role that determines what they can see and do. Roles are assigned by admins and can be changed at any time.

Global Admin

Global Admin

zanmanna team only. Full access across all organisations, all features, and all platform settings. Manages devotional content and global resources.

Org Admin

Org Admin

Your organisation's staff, counsellors, and leadership. Manages users, posts resources, responds to prayer requests, and receives crisis alerts.

Org Partner

Org Partner

Supporting churches, external organisations, or ministry partners. Can post resources and view community content. Cannot manage users or access crisis data.

Org Guest

Org Guest

Current residents of a rehabilitation center or members of a church or fellowship community. Can view all content, post prayer requests, journal privately, and trigger emergency support.

Org Past Guest

Org Past Guest

Role designed for rehabilitation context alumni who have completed a rehabilitation programme. Same access as Org Guest, plus additional alumni-focused features as they become available post-launch.

Revoked

Revoked

Access paused. The member sees a personalised message and can request reinstatement. All data is preserved. Reversal is immediate when authorised.
