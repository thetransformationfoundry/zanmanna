The Name

More than a name.
A declaration.

זָן
Zan
Present tense Hebrew for "he nourishes, he feeds" — from the root זון (zun), the source of mazon (מזון), the word for food. God is the One who nourishes all.
+
מָן
Manna
The miraculous bread from heaven God provided in the wilderness. Daily provision, grace, and true sustenance. (Exodus 16)
=
zanmanna
"Sustaining Manna" — divine nourishment for body, mind, and spirit. Our daily provision found in Jesus, the true bread of life. John 6:35

Who We Are

Fuelled by love.
Built with purpose.

zanmanna is a non-profit on a mission to spread the good news and help people find, build and grow in community through technology that doesn't replace but supports building healthy communities. We don't just build apps and digital solutions. We connect people to finding hope, family, love and freedom as we follow Jesus Christ together.


From recovery centres to churches to NGOs, wherever community is forming, we want to help it flourish. Because true discipleship is formed together.

Love
It's our foundation. Everything we build — every feature, every decision, every line of code — flows from love.
Faith

We truly believe that whoever knocks, the door will be opened. We build technology on the hope we receive from love and faith.

Our Mission

The manna in the wilderness pointed forward to something greater. Jesus is the true bread from heaven, given freely to us so that we can find life and hunger no more. zanmanna exists to help those who are hungry and thirst to find Jesus.

Inside the App

For community,

Come see what's inside Manna.

Community Wall

Spiritual resources, helpful guides, programme materials and community updates curated by your leaders and delivered based on your role. Everyone sees what is most relevant to them. The most helpful resources, available at the right time.

Daily Bread

Daily devotionals with scripture, reflection and prayer. A moment of stillness and nourishment to start each day together.

Private Journal

A safe, private space for personal reflection and growth. Voice-to-text enabled. Visible only to the person who wrote it.

Prayer Wall

A shared prayer wall where community members lift each other up. Anonymity protected, answered prayers celebrated.

Full Bible

The full Word of God in your pocket. All 66 books, 1,189 chapters and 31,103 verses. Always available, always free.

Emergency Help

One-tap support connecting community members to pastoral care. Dignity preserved in the hardest moments.
