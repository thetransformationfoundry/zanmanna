Who We Are

Fuelled by love.
Built with purpose.

ZanManna is a redemptive technology startup rooted in faith — boldly embracing who we are in Christ while embodying His example of unconditional love.


We partner with recovery centres, churches, and pastoral teams to create digital environments that honour dignity, protect privacy, and cultivate hope. Just as Christ reached out to everyone with grace and truth, we are committed to supporting every person on their recovery journey — regardless of background or belief.


"A digital table where all are welcome, all are seen, and all are nourished."

Dignity
Every person is seen, named, and honoured — never reduced to a status or a number.
Privacy
Personal reflections are sacred. We build with protection, not extraction.
Hope
Every screen, every word, every interaction points toward restoration.
Community
Recovery is not a solo journey. We build tools for communities who carry each other.

Inside the App

Everything a recovery
community needs.

Daily Bread

Daily devotionals with scripture, reflection, and prayer. A moment of stillness to start each day.

Prayer Wall

Community prayer with anonymity protection, "I'm Praying" counters, and answered prayer tracking.

Private Journal

A safe, private space for reflection. Voice-to-text enabled. Visible only to the person who wrote it.

Full Bible

All 66 books, 1,189 chapters, and 31,105 verses — always available, always free.

Resources

Recovery guides, workshop summaries, and spiritual growth materials curated by staff.

Emergency Help

One-tap crisis support connecting guests to pastoral care — dignity preserved in the hardest moments.
